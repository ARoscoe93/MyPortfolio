About Me

From unlocking phones and repairing computers and game consoles, my journey in the world of technology began with a fascination for the inner workings of digital devices. As a self-taught enthusiast, I honed my skills through countless hours of exploration, troubleshooting, and hands-on experience. With each successful repair and every problem solved, my passion for technology grew, propelling me forward on a path of continuous learning and innovation.

After achieving an NVQ Level 2 certification in gas membrane installation, I embarked on a successful decade-long career as a gas membrane technician. During this time, I not only honed my technical expertise in the field but also took on additional responsibilities as a project manager. As a project manager, I oversaw and executed numerous complex projects, ensuring their smooth implementation and timely completion. While working in the construction industry, I developed a keen eye for detail, meticulous planning skills, and a commitment to delivering high-quality results.

Life took an unexpected turn when I experienced a seizure that prompted me to reevaluate my career path. It was during this introspective period that I realized my true passion lay in coding, app development, and game creation. Motivated by the desire to turn my long-held dream into a reality, I embarked on a journey to acquire the necessary skills and knowledge to become a junior developer.

Now, armed with determination and a thirst for growth, I am actively pursuing my dream career as a junior developer. I am eager to immerse myself in the world of coding, collaborating with talented teams, and pushing the boundaries of what can be achieved through technology. With my diverse background, I bring a unique blend of problem-solving skills, technical expertise, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Join me on this exciting journey as we navigate the ever-evolving landscape of software development and transform ideas into digital realities.
